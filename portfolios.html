Why Buy and Hold?

Peter Lynch, the legendary manager of Fidelity's Magellan fund, returned 29% annually during his tenure but the average investor lost money in his fund . Why does this happen? People jump in and out of funds trying different strategy to beat the market. People also chase performance. They sell out of a fund and buy into a fund that had a year of great returns. However, those great performing funds generally will revert to the mean and underperform. You want to "buy low and sell high" but performance chasing often is a "buy high and sell low" strategy. Buy and Hold portfolios are the easiest to implement, follow, and leads to greater returns. These portfolios use index funds as data shows, they beat their actively managed counterparts.
